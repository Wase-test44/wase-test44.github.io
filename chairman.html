Meet the Chairman

Alhaji Babangida Ibrahim Dauda

Alhaji Babangida Ibrahim Dauda serves as the Chairman of the AREWA Progressive Eagle Movement. A visionary leader and passionate advocate for community development, Alhaji Dauda brings a wealth of experience in grassroots mobilization, public service, and humanitarian outreach across Northern Nigeria.

Under his stewardship, the organization has witnessed tremendous growth, expanded its reach across all 19 Northern states and the FCT, and implemented programs that address critical social, environmental, and economic challenges. His deep commitment to the AREWA vision and values continues to inspire coordinators, volunteers, and partners to work toward a united and thriving Northern Nigeria.

Known for his humility, discipline, and relentless pursuit of positive impact, Chairman Dauda embodies the spirit of leadership that defines the Movement's progress. He remains a strong voice for youth empowerment, cultural preservation, environmental sustainability, and peacebuilding across the region.
