Meet the Secretary

The Secretary of the AREWA Progressive Eagle Movement is a key pillar in the daily coordination, communication, and administrative operations of the organization. Serving with dedication and efficiency, the Secretary ensures that all strategic plans and grassroots programs are well-documented, monitored, and executed effectively.

With a keen eye for organization and an unwavering commitment to the mission, the Secretary plays a critical role in building strong institutional memory and accountability within the movement. From coordinating inter-state communication to managing official records, the Secretary remains the backbone of internal order and project tracking.

As a strong advocate for unity, structure, and inclusive leadership, the Secretary continues to support the leadership team, ensuring transparency and progress as AREWA Progressive Eagle Movement expands its impact across Northern Nigeria.
