Welcome to AREWA Progressive Eagle Movement

Empowering Northern Nigeria for a sustainable future.

Our Philosophy

We believe in empowering communities through self-reliance, education, and cultural pride.

Aims & Objectives

  • Promote sustainable development
  • Foster youth engagement and empowerment
  • Preserve cultural heritage
  • Promoting Corporate Social Responsibilities
  • Combating Desertification through Environmental Protection Programmes
  • Harnessing Natural Resources and Increasing or Improving Their Value Chains
  • Promoting AREWA Rich Cultural Heritage
  • Renewing the Mindset of people towards future Reality from their Immediate Environment or Community
  • Assisting with Peace Building Programmes

We organize community development projects, environmental campaigns, and educational seminars.
